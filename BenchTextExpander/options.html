Message Macros

Edit your database of shortcuts and expansions here! Useful tips.

  • Date/time macros are supported! Wrap your date format with %d(dateFormat). Please see this for how to format dates in your expanded text. Example: %d(MMMM Do YYYY), and would expand to something like: September 4rd 2013.
  • Backing up your shortcuts with the "Backup" button only makes an extra copy of them in Chrome's local storage quota. This means if Chrome's syncing gets messed up, you can restore from your backup, but if you sign out of your browser or uninstall the extension, you will lose your backup. You can get a copy of your shortcuts from the Import/Export dialog.
